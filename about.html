Our story

From a small startup to a regional leader

It all started in 2015 when our founders — two tech enthusiasts — opened a tiny 20 m² shop on Stefan cel Mare Boulevard. Back then we stocked 50 products and delivered orders by bicycle.

By 2018 we launched our first warehouse and in-house delivery fleet, cutting delivery times to 24 hours across Chișinău. We expanded into Ukraine the following year, opening fulfillment hubs in Kyiv and Odessa.

Today Brand operates 4 retail locations, an e-commerce platform visited by over 300,000 people monthly, and a dedicated service center staffed by certified engineers. We remain proudly independent — no outside investors, no compromises on quality.
